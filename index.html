Malaphor generator

A 'malaphor' is when two figures of speech are merged, like combining "It's not brain surgery" and "It's not rocket science" to get "It's not rocket surgery."

This command-line tool was made in Python. It generates malaphors based on proverbs, with priority given to semantically similar sayings.
